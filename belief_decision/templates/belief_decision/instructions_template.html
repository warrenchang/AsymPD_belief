The Number of Rounds

The match consists of a number of rounds. The exact number is randomly determined by the rolling of a die.

At the end of each round, a die with twenty sides is rolled. If the numbers 1-16 appear, the match will continue for another round. If the numbers 17-20 appear, the match ends.

In other words, after one round is played, the chance that the match will continue for another round is 16 out of 20.
